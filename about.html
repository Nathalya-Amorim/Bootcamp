ABOUT ME

I’m Nathalya Menezes, a Front-End Developer and UI / UX from Brazil but currently based in London; passionate about creating and discovering new things with JavaScript, HTML and CSS. How did I get into tech? In Brazil, I’ve worked as a kindergarten teacher. However, after ten years of teaching kids, I’ve realized that teaching is no longer my passion. Understanding technology and learning new things related to the web have always been a hobby. Naturally, tech was a perfect place for me; it is where I’ve decided to start my new career. Initially, I had no idea what I was doing, but I dived right in. I began with an HTML short course at Udemy; then, I took a more significant step by studying Interactive Media Design at Fanshawe College. I must admit that studying technology is not always a pleasure and requires much commitment, and facing constant challenges is tough. But I will also say that seeing me overcome obstacles and learn and debate solutions is exciting and motivating.
