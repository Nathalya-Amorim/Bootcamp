ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Seunghyeon Yeom, and I currently live in London, Ontario, Canada. I'm a designer and front-end developer who is constantly exploring for new perspectives. I'm very interested in website ux/ui design.
After taking the interactive media design course at Fanshawe College, I became interested in website design and development. Throughout the course, I learned about several types of design, such as graphic design, ux/ui design, motion design, and so on. It's interesting to think about how to deliver website material, that is both accessible and visually appealing, to the users.
In addition, I have studied web development and particularly enjoy front-end development. I believe that the front-end is a part of design because there are numerous ways to make the website look fancy by using CSS and Javascript. CSS is certainly interesting to me, and I aspire to be the one who can improve this field.
